tooltip

A lightweight, easy, straight-forward and very simple tooltip made with jQuery

Basic usage

Hover me

<span data-tooltip="Hello world">Hover me</span>;
<button class="button" data-tooltip="You can apply tooltip to any element">A button with a tooltip</button>

Placement

Default tooltip will show on top of the element

Tooltip at the right

Tooltip at the bottom

Tooltip at the left

<span data-tooltip="Tooltip at the top">Default tooltip will show on top of the element</span>
<span data-tooltip="On your right" data-placement="right">Tooltip at the right</span>
<span data-tooltip="I'm here at the bottom" data-placement="bottom">Tooltip at the bottom</span>
<span data-tooltip="On your left" data-placement="left">Tooltip at the left</span>

Distance

Tooltip will show 20px away from me

<span data-tooltip="Hello world" data-distance="20">Tooltip will show 20px away from me</span>

Custom styling via css class

Tooltip with primary class

A success tooltip

An info tooltip

A warning tooltip

An error tooltip

<span data-tooltip="Yay, I can have a style!" data-class="primary">Tooltip with primary class</span>
<span data-tooltip="Success!" data-class="success">A success tooltip</span>
<span data-tooltip="Info here..." data-class="info">An info tooltip</span>
<span data-tooltip="Warning! Warning!" data-class="warning">A warning tooltip</span>
<span data-tooltip="Oooooooops..." data-class="error">An error tooltip</span>
